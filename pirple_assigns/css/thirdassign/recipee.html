Muffin Cake

This Muffin cake is literally a 2-ingridient recipe and is so easy to make that even a 6 year old can make it.
It is really tasty at the same time. Its the perfect recipe for choco-lovers who are beginners in baking.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Nutella

Steps

  1. Put 3 scoops of nutella in a bowl🍫
  2. Separate the egg yolk from the egg white🥚.
  3. Put the yolk in the nutella and mix well.
  4. Beat the egg whites till it becomes cream-like.🍦
  5. Add the cream into the nutella mixture and mix well.🥣
  6. Put the mixture into a mould and put it into the oven.
  7. Take it out after 15 mins.🧁

Muffin Cake is Ready!!
